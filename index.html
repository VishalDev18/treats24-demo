About Treats24

Food Delivery Built with Heart & Soul

Treats24 is a community-driven food delivery platform focused on connecting people with local food culture. It aims to deliver delicious food that is accessible, affordable, and convenient while offering a seamless ordering experience. By partnering with local restaurants, cloud kitchens, and home chefs, Treats24 supports small businesses and promotes authentic neighborhood flavors. Its mission is to bring people together through great food while strengthening the local economy
